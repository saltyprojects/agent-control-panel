Frequently Asked Questions

What AI providers do you support? ‚ñº

We support all major AI providers including OpenAI (GPT-4, GPT-5), Anthropic (Claude Opus, Sonnet), Google (Gemini), and open-source models. Our platform is provider-agnostic, so you can use any combination of models for your agent team.

How do you prevent agent runaways? ‚ñº

We provide real-time monitoring with automatic killswitches based on cost thresholds, token usage, suspicious behavior, and time limits. You can set custom rules per agent, and our dashboard gives you one-click emergency stop for any agent or the entire team.

Do I need to change my existing agent code? ‚ñº

No! Agent Control Panel integrates via a lightweight SDK that wraps your existing agent code. It typically takes less than 5 minutes to integrate. We support Python, JavaScript/TypeScript, and have REST APIs for any language.

What about data privacy and security? ‚ñº

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We offer self-hosted deployment for enterprises with strict compliance requirements. Our monitoring logs only metadata (costs, tokens, tool calls), not your actual prompts or responses unless you explicitly enable detailed logging.

How is pricing calculated? ‚ñº

We charge based on the number of active agents and monitoring features enabled. The free tier includes up to 3 agents with basic monitoring. Pro plans start at $29/month for 10 agents. Enterprise pricing is custom based on scale and compliance needs. Check our pricing page for details.

Can I use this for production applications? ‚ñº

Absolutely. We're designed for production use with 99.9% uptime SLA, dedicated support, and enterprise-grade security. Many teams use us to manage agents in customer-facing applications, internal tools, and automated workflows.
