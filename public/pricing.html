Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as an "agent"? +
Any AI agent session that Agent Control Panel monitors. This includes Claude Code sessions, OpenClaw agents, custom agents using our SDK, etc.
Is there a free trial? +
The Starter plan is free forever. For Pro and Team, we offer a 14-day free trial with no credit card required.
What integrations do you support? +
We support OpenClaw, Claude Code, and any agent that can use our REST API or SDK. More integrations coming soon.
Why is self-hosted recommended? +
Self-hosted gives you complete control: your agent logs never leave your infrastructure, no per-seat SaaS fees, deploy on your existing servers, and zero vendor lock-in. Most enterprises, research labs, and security-conscious teams prefer self-hosted.
What if I prefer managed cloud? +
Our managed cloud plans (Starter, Pro, Team) are great for teams that want hosted infrastructure. We handle deployment, scaling, and maintenance. Both options give you the same powerful agent control features.
